Satoru Iwata

President and heart of Nintendo

"Satoru Iwata holding the Nintendo 3DS"



A timeline of Satoru Iwata's life and work:

  • 1959 - Born in Sapporo, Japan.
  • 1974 - Purchased his first programmable calculator.
  • 1975 - Began programming his first games including Volleyball and Missle Attack.
  • 1978 - Obtained his first computer. A Commador PET that coincidentally had the same processor that Nintendo used.
  • 1980 - While attending University he worked at HAL laboratory as a part time programmer.
  • 1983 - Became the companys coordinater of software production.
  • 1993 - Promoted to president at the insistence of then Nintendo president Hiroshi Yamauchi.
  • 1999 - Assisted on Pokemon Gold and Silver, which released for gameboy color.
  • 2000 - Took a position at Nintendo as Head of Corporate Planning Division.
  • 2002 - Succeeded as Nintendo's fourth president after Yamachui's retirement.
  • 2003 - Gave a speech at the Tokyo Game Show stating that the interest in video games was declining. That the pushing of hardware was not the way to go but to focus on the software.
  • 2004 - In an interview he stated "that video games had come to a dead end." Companies spend to much time focusing on core gamers, they wouldnt turn a profit if they didn't tend to the average player.
  • 2005 - Would later work on an expansion project, whereaby employees not normally assosiated with game development would submit game ideas.
  • 2006 - Introduced us to the Wii.
  • 2009 - At E3 introduced the new add-on for the Wii. A vitality sensor, which monitored autonomic nerve functions maily pulse. This would lead to relaxation products for the Wii.
  • 2010 - After losses in profit voulintarily halved his salary of $1.13 million. He did as an apology for the decline in sales. To ensure the jobs of employee's and keep from them being laid off.
  • 2013 - Took on the additional role of Nintendo of America's CEO.
  • 2014 - Nintendo announced that he would not be present at E3, due to the fact he had underwent surgery the previous week to remove a tumor.
  • 2015 - Passed away after becoming ill due to complications from the bile duct tumor.

For more imformation go to Satoru Iwata's Wikipedia.
